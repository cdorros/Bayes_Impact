About

MineData.org was developed at the inaugural Bayes Impact hackathon with the goal of exploring publicly-availble data about mines in the United States from the Department of Labor.

We picked this data set because of recent events involving accidents and regulation in the mining industry. Being able to objectively identify mines that are at-risk for accidents means that inspection efforts can be more effectively targeted to improve safety.
